Overview


Welcome to the Neuroimaging Data Modeling (NIDM) BIDS Specification Terms website. We aim to provide a user friendly tool that facilitates community engagement in BIDS Specification Terms through providing a simple tree-view display of the specification terms, allowing new terms submission, as well as allowing terms to be exported in various formats (e.g. as a Markdown Table).
